# Homemade Katsu Sauce

- ⅓ cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon mirin
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
