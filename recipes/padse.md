# Homemade Pad Sew Ew Sauce

- 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons white vinegar
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons water
